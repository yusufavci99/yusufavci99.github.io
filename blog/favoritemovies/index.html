My Favorite Movies

# Mar 30, 2021

All of these movies have a special place for me. My descriptions will be very short because I don’t want to spoil much.

Barry Lyndon

This movie is beautiful. I cannot comprehend how this movie was filmed in 1975. And the story was incredibly believeable.

Kill Bill

Top notch action. And if you’re not watching this because you’re afraid that it’s too gross or violent, just watch it.

Pulp Fiction

I’m not sure why I like this movie that much. But I do.

Matrix

This one doesn’t need a description.

13th Floor

Has a similar premise to Matrix but it’s an entirely different film with less action but with different, interesting ideas.

Blade Runner 2049 (and Blade Runner)

Another beautiful looking movie. It’s worth watching only for the visuals. It’s very original too.

A Clockwork Orange

I didn’t fully appreciate this movie when I first watched it. But it explores interesting some interesting philosophy. The unfamiliar setting is incredible too.

The Invention Of Lying

That’s a very smart and funny movie
